Tips, Notes and Special remark
  • Wear attire suiting cold climates
  • It's better if you can carry a stock of water (1L per person) and food products.
  • The slop is at the direction of Ohiya to Kalupahana. So it is recommend to start the hike from Ohiya. If you are start it from Kalupahana side it will be very hard to climb up and walk around 30Km. If you are using an off road vehicle this is not relevant.
  • I personally recommend use earlier day night mail to come to Ohiya. It will be amazing experience for you.
  • Start as early as possible( 8 hour journey on foot / To complete the whole trail at least you should start before sunrise or you could use )
  • Do not carry Plastic items (Minimize), Make sure you bring back everything you took. Leave only foot prints.
  • Ask directions from locals
  • No leeches
  • Avoid overcast conditions(better to go there during Nuwara Eliya season December to May)

Trail details

This is one of best trail in Sri Lanka in central province which is the road connecting Ohiya and Kalupahana. The road itself like a staircase. That's why it got the name. The road is kind scary but the pathway with beautiful sceneries will blow your mid. Â It's about 25 plus Km to Ohiya to Kalupahana. You can travel either by foot or vehicle. If you can't get overly tired a vehicle would be the best option. Its nearly 4 hours for an off road vehicle and for a bike ride it will take less than that. But you are an explorer I recommend walk. It will take less than 8 hrs.

Eelier day we were very excited to start our journey from the Peradeniya railway station. At 11.10PM we took the night mail and after 6 hours journey we reached to Ohiya railway station early in the morning.

We started walking at 5AM from the station finding this glorious path, after couple of hours (1 -2Hrs) we were walking through the Devil's Staircase. The path is running along the middle edge of a mountain to the foot of it and also faced to a large open area with couple of mountains. And you will also see couple of waterfalls falling down from the top of those mountains. Best thing is the sun rise through the mountains into the misty clouds (At around 6-7 AM), I must say that scenery which will blow your mind. You will find some isolated village in the path if it possible take some sweets for the children.

The villagers are humble and innocent. Greet them when passing.

Please don't take any plastics or anything that you bought which could harm the beauty of it. Leave it as it is. Leave only foot prints.
