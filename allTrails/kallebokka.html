Trail details

Kallebokka is an estate in Panwila and its highest peak which is directly accessible by vehicles is a scenic view point offering a 360 view of the surrounding.

The place is quiet easy to find as there is a sign marked "Kallebokka Upper Division 360 View point" as you travel along the Kandy - Kabaragala road.

From this point onward its a road of about 6 km uphill to reach the view point.

The road is not suitable for all vehicles and therefore bikes or 4 wheel driven vehicles are recommended.

Then to reach the peak you have to purchase a 100 Rs ticket. The surrounding view is magnificent with tea estates and distant mountain ranges including the Knuckles. Its is rare to have a clear 360 view of the surrounding at the peak of amt mountain peak and its even more rare for the peak to be directly accessible by vehicles.

Its is a great camping spot to spend a night you can observe the sunrise and the sunset over the mountains.

The unmatched view offers a great chance for beautiful photographs and there are some straw thatched huts built to add to the beauty. Be sure to visit here in a sunny day as if the area is misty, the trip will be of no use. Kallebokka is a place that must be visited by anyone who wishes to see the natural beauty of Sri Lanka.
