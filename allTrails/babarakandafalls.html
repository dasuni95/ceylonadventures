Trail details

This is the tallest waterfall in Sri Lanka with a sheer drop of 240 meters. The location of the waterfall is at the end of the Devil's staircase path. So it is recommended to make the visit part of the Devil’s staircase trail. Or for the visitors who willing to visit the waterfall directly, it is recommended to come to Kalupahana town first (which is located in the Badulla – Colombo highway, 30Km away from Walimada town) and then few kilometers along the Ohiya road.

If you are coming from Ohiya (Devil's staircase root), the entrance is at the left side of the road and you ll have to walk little bit into the wood to the foot of the waterfall. And you can also see it from the top of it but you have to take permission. For the visitors have to pay Small amount for the entrance ticket. For the tourists it is included tourist price which is bit different then the local's .
