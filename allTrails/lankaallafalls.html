Trail details

This is also one of beautiful waterfalls which neighbor to Bambarakanda waterfall. The beauty of it is not second to Bambarakanda waterfall. The location of it kind of far away from it into the woods (3-5Km from the foot of Bambarakanda waterfall). And the path is kind of scary but no doubt the risk is worth to see the beauty of it. You would have to walk through a groove of pine trees to the top of a mountain which is beside the Bmbarakanda waterfall. Once you get to the top you will have a clear site of beautiful scenery. And the path from there is clearly visible. When you walk along the path which let along a fully grown grassy area, it will start to hear the sound of the water falling. There also some other path which are coming from the top of the Devil's staircase root but they are more dangerous and not recommended. But still some of people are using those paths too.
